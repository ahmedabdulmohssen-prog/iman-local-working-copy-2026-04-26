## Major Lessons
- Backend math must control outputs
- AI should explain, not calculate
- Users reject unrealistic recommendations
- Users will not pay for obvious advice only
- Simpler onboarding wins
- Trustworthy results matter more than flashy features
- Granular controllable savings matter more than generic budgeting
